Welcome to Gmail Organizer

Gmail Organizer is a Chrome extension that helps you manage your Gmail inbox by creating and applying customizable sorting rules. With Gmail Organizer, you can keep your inbox tidy and organized effortlessly.

Features

  • Create sorting rules based on email author, subject, or content.
  • Automatically move emails to specified folders.
  • Review and manage existing sorting rules.

Get started by installing the extension and setting up your sorting rules!
